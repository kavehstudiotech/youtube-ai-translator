Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension (Persian YouTube Translator) handles your data.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data, credentials, or browsing history to our own servers. All translation settings, custom API keys, and configuration preferences are stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure local storage API.

2. Third-Party Services

The extension sends subtitle text directly to third-party translation providers (such as Google Translate, Google AI Studio, OpenAI, DeepSeek, or Grok) solely for the purpose of translation. This data transfer happens directly from your browser to the respective API endpoints. We do not have access to, nor do we store, any of this data.

3. Security of API Keys

Your API keys (if used) are stored locally on your computer and are never shared with us or any unauthorized third parties.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
